Fly High

Be sure your kite is put together correctly, or it may not fly. Deltas, Diamonds and Dragon kites fly well in light to medium winds (approximately 6-15 mph) while Box Kites and stickless Parafoil kites fly better when the winds get a little stronger (approximately 8-25 mph). Equip your self with the right kite and your bound to dominate the sky.
